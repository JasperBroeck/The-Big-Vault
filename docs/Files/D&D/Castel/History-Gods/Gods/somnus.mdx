# Somnus
![image](/assets/gods/Somnus.jpg)

- Domains:
    - Dreams
    - Neutral Magic
    - Elements
- Description:
    - Somnus is the divine entity responsible for the realm of dreams and the balance of neutral magic. As the Dreamweaver, they control the ebb and flow of dreams, weaving them into the tapestry of reality. As the keeper of neutral magic, Somnus ensures equilibrium among the elemental forces, preventing any one element from dominating the others.
