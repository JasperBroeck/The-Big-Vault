These are still reanimated and corrupted, but they'll be weaker for a more balanced fight.

    Armor Class: 12 (Natural Armor)
    Hit Points: 15 (3d8 + 3)
    Speed: 40 ft.
    STR: 16 (+3), DEX: 10 (+0), CON: 14 (+2), INT: 2 (-4), WIS: 10 (+0), CHA: 5 (-3)
    Damage Resistances: Necrotic, Psychic
    Senses: Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 12
    Languages: Understands Common, but can't speak

Abilities:

    Charge: If the cow moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and hits it with a hoof attack, the target must succeed on a DC 11 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
    Hooves: Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage.
    Shadowy Charge: When the cow charges, it can move through creatures' spaces, as long as they are no more than 1 size larger than the cow.

    Speed: 40 ft.
    STR: 16 (+3), DEX: 11 (+0), CON: 14 (+2), INT: 2 (-4), WIS: 11 (+0), CHA: 6 (-2)
    Damage Resistances: Necrotic, Psychic
    Senses: Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 10
    Languages: Understands Common, but can't speak

    Mighty Charge: If the bison moves at least 20 feet in a straight line toward a creature and hits it with horns, the target must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
