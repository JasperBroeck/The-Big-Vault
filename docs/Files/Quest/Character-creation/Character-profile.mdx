# Character profile
This is what the character profile
sounds like:

Hello, my name is (name)
(pronouns). I’m (age) years old and
stand (height) tall.

1: I’m the party’s (role).

2: When people see me, they
first notice my (distinctive feature
1), (distinctive feature 2), and
(distinctive feature 3).

3: I wear (clothing item 1), (clothing
item 2), and move with (movement
style).

4: I’m from (home), where my
people are known for (community
feature).

5: I believe in (ideal),

6: but my (flaw) side can get in my
way.

7: I dream of (dream).

8: I carry (use your character
worksheet).
